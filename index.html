Description of the Course

The purpose of this course is to provide an introduction to physics, with focus on concepts,
(while using Elementary Algebra) as a tool to guide in the understanding of everyday life.
Will cover Newtonian Mechanics and Electricity & Magnetism.

Make-Up Exams Policy

All tests must be taken on the scheduled date.
NO Make-up exams will be given. Make sure you do not have any schedule conflict.
  • If any of the first two exams is missed, it will be dropped and its weight (10%) will
    be counted towards your second highest exam score for a total weight of 30%.

  • If a second exam is missed, 30% of your final grade will be dropped
    (highest final grade you can get is 70%).

  • If the final exam is missed, it will result in FAILURE.

Homework Assignments

Homework assignments will be posted on a weekly basis and students are expected
to complete them within 5-7 days. The HW will be graded as follows:

On the HW due date, 4 students will be called at random to solve any one of the HW problems
on the board within 3 minutes (without notes). This will constitute 20% of the total grade.
A student may be called more than once during the semester.

While collaboration on homework assignments is allowed, simply copying work from another
student is NOT allowed and will be considered cheating.
